Happiness

An apocalyptic thriller that takes place in a time in which infectious diseases have become the new normal.

 
        Cast
Park Hyung-sik, Han Hyo-joo, 
Jo Woo-jin
Director: Ahn Gil-ho
Episodes: 14

Personal Review: Enjoyable watch, the fact that the people are the main threat rather than the infected makes the show much more interesting to me. Overall, 9.5/10. (The ending confused me)

My Demon

A pitiless demon becomes powerless after getting entangled with an icy heiress, who may hold the key to his lost abilities -- and his heart.

 
        Cast
Song Kang, Kim Yoo-jung, 
Kim Tae-hoon
Director: Kim Jang Han
Episodes: 16

Personal Review: Pretty cliche, I could predict the events of the next episodes especially due to the supernatural powers present in the film, but the dynamic between the two lead actors is too good to not watch the show. Overall, 8/10

Soundtrack 1

Best friends for almost twenty years, our two main characters can no longer ignore the feelings blooming between them as they start living together.

 
        Cast
Han Hyo-joo, Park Hyung-sik,
Director: Kim Hee-won
Episodes: 4

Personal Review: A short, sweet friends-to-lovers story with great leads. Would recommend watching if you aren't trying to watch anything with heavy conflicts. Overall, 9/10.

Once Upon A Boyhood

Set against the backdrop of the mid-to-late 1980s. Suspense drama of students at an agricultural high school in Chungcheong Province.

 
        Cast
Im Si-wan, Lee Sun-bin, 
Lee Sang-jin
Director: Lee Myoungwoo
Episodes: 10

Personal Review: Funny revenge story with satisfying fight scenes and romance scenes. Overall, 9/10
